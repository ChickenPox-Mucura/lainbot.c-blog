Day 4

Today i learned something very useful, althought i won't need it any time soon. It is about traffic; about driving.

First of all i don't like the idea about driving very much. I was never amused by it, except sometimes when i see an old car of my liking (think about a Volkswagen Kombi or a Brazilian Gurgel for example). Perhaps it's not the driving itself, it may be the (excruciating) process of getting a license instead. Anyway, what i learned is that if you're driving a bus, and are about to take a turn in a narrow street in which another bus is coming towards you, instead of actually carefully taking the turn, you should go straight or backwards and then wait for the other bus to go on it's way and only then you can go forward or backwards again and proceed to turning.

Anyway folks, that's it for today.
